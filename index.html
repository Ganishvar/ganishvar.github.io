Springer Conference Paper

Development of Low-Cost ECU Using CAN Bus Protocol

Ganishvar S, et al.
Presented and published at the Springer International Conference 2025.

Abstract & Overview

Modern automotive platforms rely on numerous complex Electronic Control Units (ECUs) communicating via Controller Area Network (CAN) buses. This paper proposes a low-cost, open-source architecture for a vehicle ECU utilizing an STM32 microcontroller for high-speed sensor control and a Raspberry Pi 4 for localized data diagnostics. Implementing MCP2515 controllers and TJA1040 transceivers, we showcase robust error containment, message priorities, and remote diagnostic telemetry under simulated EMI noise conditions.
